Carbonara

Go back to Odin Recipes

Cook: 20 mins

Total: 35 mins

Prep: 15 mins

Servings: 8

Yield: 8 servings

Description

Truly authentic Italian carbonara.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • 12 slices thick-cut, jowls
  • Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 16 egg yolks
  • Pepper at taste

How to make it?

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, place chopped jowls in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain jowls chops on paper towels. Reserve jowls fat in the skillet.
  3. Mix egg yolks and Pecorino Romano cheese and add some Pepper at taste. Add some coocking water and some of the jowls fat.
  4. Drain spaghetti, transfer into a bowl with the remaining jowls fat. Add chopped jowls and the mix of eggs and cheese; stir until pasta is well coated and creamy; add some more coocking water if necessary.
